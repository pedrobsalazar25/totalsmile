## 🎯 How to Download from Pexels

```
1. Click the "→ Download" link
2. You'll go to the Pexels page
3. Click the blue "Free Download" button
4. Choose resolution (HD or 4K)
5. File saves to your computer as .mp4 or .jpg
6. Rename to match scene (e.g., "scene1_costa_sunset.mp4")
7. Upload to HeyGen
```
