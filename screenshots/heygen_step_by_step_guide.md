## BEFORE YOU START

### What You Need Ready
- [ ] Pexels B-roll clips downloaded (8 scenes)

## STEP 1 — Log In & Start New Video

```
🎯 ACTION: Open HeyGen and create new video
```

1. Go to **heygen.com**
2. Click **"Log In"** (top right) → Sign in with Google or email
3. You're now on the HeyGen dashboard

---

## STEP 2 — Create New Video

```
🎯 ACTION: Start the video creation process
```

1. Click the **"+" (Create Video)** button (large, blue or purple)
2. Select **"Video"** (not "Image" or "Shorts")
3. Choose your **Aspect Ratio**:
   - For mobile/social → Select **"9:16 Vertical"**
   - For YouTube → Select **"16:9"**
4. Click **"Create"** or **"Start Creating"**

---

## STEP 3 — Select Your AI Avatar

```
🎯 ACTION: Choose the presenter avatar
```

### Option A: Built-in AI Avatar (Recommended for Free)
1. On the left sidebar → Click **"Select Avatar"**
2. Browse the **"AI Avatars"** tab
3. Use the **search box** or **filter** to find a professional female avatar:
   - Search: "professional woman" or "business woman"
   - Filter: **"Female"** + **"Standing"** or **"Upper body"**
4. **Preview** the avatar — click on it to see how they look and move
5. Click the avatar you want → Click **"Use Avatar"**
6. Position the avatar:
   - In the preview canvas, drag the avatar to the **lower third** (bottom of screen)
   - This leaves room for B-roll to show above

### Option B: Use a Talking Photo (Your Own Image)
1. Click **"Use Talking Photo"**
2. Upload a **professional stock photo** of a woman (or use Pexels photo)
   - Search Pexels: "professional woman business portrait"
3. HeyGen will animate the photo with lip sync

## STEP 5 — Configure Voice Settings

```
🎯 ACTION: Set the voice speed and enable lip sync
```

1. Find the **voice settings** (usually near the script box)
2. **Select Voice:**
   - Click the voice dropdown
   - Search for: **"Rachel"**, **"Sarah"**, or **"Emma"** (warm, professional)
   - Preview the voice first
   - Click to select
3. **Set Speed:** `0.9x` (slightly slower = luxury feel)
4. **Toggle "Lip Sync" ON** ✅
5. **Toggle "Subtitles"** (optional — turn ON if you want captions)

---

## STEP 6 — Add Background B-Roll (Scene by Scene)

```
🎯 ACTION: Upload your downloaded Pexels clips as background videos
```

### How HeyGen Scenes Work:
HeyGen uses a **timeline** where you add scenes. Each scene = avatar talking + B-roll background.

### For Each Scene, Do This:

#### Scene 1 — Opening (Costa Rica Drone)
1. Click **"Add Scene"** or **"+"** on the timeline
2. Click **"Add Media"** → **"Upload"**
3. Select your downloaded file: `scene1_costa_sunset.mp4`
4. The video now appears as the background
5. Adjust the scene duration: ~5 seconds

#### Scene 2 — Arrival (Airport Transfer)
1. Click **"Add Scene"**
2. Upload: `scene2_airport_transfer.mp4`
3. Duration: ~7 seconds

#### Scene 3 — Alajuela Clinic
1. Click **"Add Scene"**
2. Upload: `scene3_dental_clinic.mp4`
3. Duration: ~8 seconds

#### Scene 4 — Procedures
1. Click **"Add Scene"**
2. Upload: `scene4_dental_procedure.mp4`
3. Duration: ~12 seconds

#### Scene 5 — Mountain Recovery
1. Click **"Add Scene"**
2. Upload: `scene5_mountain_valley.mp4`
3. Duration: ~13 seconds

#### Scene 6 — Playas del Coco (Hero Shot)
1. Click **"Add Scene"**
2. Upload: `scene6_ocean_view.mp4`
3. Duration: ~13 seconds

#### Scene 7 — Concierge Tours
1. Click **"Add Scene"**
2. Upload: `scene7_volcano.mp4` (or cloud forest / beach — use 3 clips if possible)
3. Duration: ~10 seconds

#### Scene 8 — Transformation & CTA
1. Click **"Add Scene"**
2. Upload: `scene8_smile_transformation.mp4` (or use a photo)
3. Duration: ~12 seconds
4. Add end card with logo + contact info (HeyGen has text tool)

## STEP 7 — Preview Your Video

```
🎯 ACTION: Review the full video before exporting
```

1. Click the **"Preview"** button (usually large, center or top right)
2. Watch the full video with:
   - ✅ Avatar lip syncing
   - ✅ B-roll playing behind
   - ✅ Timing feels right
3. **Adjust timing** if needed:
   - Drag scene edges to extend/shorten
   - Make sure avatar finishes speaking before scene ends
4. **Adjust avatar position** if B-roll has important content in the middle

## STEP 9 — Export & Download

```
🎯 ACTION: Render and download your video
```

1. Click the **"Export"** or **"Publish"** button
2. Wait for HeyGen to render (1-3 minutes usually)
3. When done, click **"Download"**
4. Video saves to your computer as **.mp4**

## 🎯 HEYGEN FREE TIPS & GOTCHAS

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| **Watermark on export** | HeyGen Free adds a small watermark — upgrade to remove, or accept it |
| **3-minute limit** | ~90-second video fits comfortably in your free minutes |
| **Avatar looks robotic** | Choose a "Neural" avatar (more natural movement) |
| **B-roll not showing** | Make sure you clicked "Add Media" and selected the video file |
| **Lip sync off** | Wait for full render — sometimes preview looks off but export is fine |
| **Audio cuts out early** | Extend the scene duration slightly past the voiceover length |

## ❓ TROUBLESHOOTING

**"I don't see the B-roll options"**
→ Make sure you're in **"Video"** mode (not "Shorts" or "Image")

**"The avatar is blocking the B-roll"**
→ Drag the avatar in the preview canvas to reposition — put it in lower third

**"Lip sync looks wrong"**
→ The preview may look glitchy but the export render is usually better. Try different avatar.

**"I ran out of free minutes"**
→ HeyGen Free gives ~3 minutes. Your 90-second video uses 1.5 minutes. Wait for free credits to reset, or upgrade.
