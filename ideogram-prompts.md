# The Smile Sanctuary — Image Prompts for Ideogram

## Overview

You have real photos of clinics, doctors, and patients — those will be used in the actual site. These Ideogram prompts are for **placeholder/generic imagery** that can be swapped in during the Lovable build, or used as decorative backgrounds.

**Note:** All prompts use a **warm, luxurious, photorealistic** style to match the white + warm wood + ocean blues aesthetic.

## Tips for Ideogram

1. **Start with the hero (21:9)** — that's the most impactful
2. **For cards:** You could also just use icons instead of full images — icons in Light Oak/Coral colors may be cleaner and load faster
3. **For real photos:** Once you have your real clinic/doctor photos, replace the placeholder cards with those — they will be much more authentic
4. **Style consistency:** All prompts use "warm golden hour," "luxury," "photorealistic" to keep a cohesive feel
5. **No people in backgrounds** — keeps focus on text/CTAs and feels premium/spa-like

---

## Recommended Priority

If you only create a few, do this order:
1. **Hero background** (most important — sets the mood)
2. **Recovery background** (second most visual)
3. Optional: Location card images if you don't have real photos yet
